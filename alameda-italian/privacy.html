Privacy

Isola — Alameda Eats is a static website. We do not run user accounts and we do not collect personal information on our servers. Two things are stored locally in your own browser (not sent to us): your saved favorites and your light/dark theme choice. You can clear them anytime by clearing your browser storage.

If you submit your email to our newsletter, it is used only to send occasional updates about Alameda dining, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

Cookies & advertising

This site may display advertising. If advertising is enabled, third-party vendors — including Google — may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Affiliate & advertising disclosure

Some outbound links on this site — for example "Order" or "Reserve" buttons — may be affiliate or referral links. If you book, order, or sign up through one of them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects which restaurants we list or how they're ranked.

"Featured" listings are paid placements purchased by the restaurant and are always labeled "Featured." All other listings are editorial and are not pay-to-rank. We aim to keep the guide trustworthy first.

Accuracy

Hours, menus, and prices are provided for convenience, are approximate, and change frequently. Always confirm directly with the restaurant. To report an error, email StevensLegacyStudios@gmail.com.
