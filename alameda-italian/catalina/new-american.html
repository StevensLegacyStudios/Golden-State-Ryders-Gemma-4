Avalon Grille $$$

Descanso Beach Club $$

★ 4.4 · New American · 1 St. Catherine Way, Avalon, CA 90704

The Naughty Fox $$

★ 4.2 · New American · 111 Crescent Ave, Avalon, CA 90704

Trendy little bar-kitchen under the Bellanca Hotel with sidewalk high-tops facing the bay — the ahi poke nachos and mini lobster rolls have a real following. Kitchen runs Thu–Mon and stops at 2pm; the bar pours till 6.
