Coyote Joe's $$

★ 4.0 · Mexican · 113 Catalina Ave, Avalon, CA 90704

Harbor Reef Restaurant & Saloon $$

★ 4.2 · American · 1 Banning House Rd, Two Harbors, CA 90704

The one real restaurant in Two Harbors — if the swordfish flag flies on the pier, local swordfish is in the kitchen. The attached saloon is where the Buffalo Milk cocktail was invented 40+ years ago; bar opens at 3pm daily.

Marlin Club $

★ 4.4 · Bar & Lounge · 108 Catalina Ave, Avalon, CA 90704

Avalon's oldest bar — built like a boat's bow by Merchant Marines during WWII, with Bud Upton's 1946 underwater mural still on the walls. Stiff drinks, pool, darts, zero pretension; open till 2am when the rest of town sleeps.

★ 4.6 · Movies & Shows · 1 Casino Way, Avalon, CA 90704
