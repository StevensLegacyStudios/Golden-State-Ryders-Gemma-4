Catalina Casino $$

★ 4.7 · Attraction · 1 Casino Way, Avalon, CA 90704

Avalon's 1929 Art Deco crown jewel — never a gambling hall, but home to the world's largest circular ballroom. Book the walking or behind-the-scenes tour; times sell out on cruise-ship days.
