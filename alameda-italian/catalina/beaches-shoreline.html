Two Harbors $

★ 4.7 · Beach & Shoreline · 1 Banning House Rd (Visitor Services), Two Harbors, CA 90704

Catalina's one-restaurant, no-stoplight west-end outpost on a half-mile isthmus between two coves — the anti-Avalon. Ferry in via Catalina Express or hike the TCT; the Dive & Rec shop rents snorkel gear, paddleboards and bikes.

Lovers Cove Marine Preserve $

★ 4.6 · Beach & Shoreline · 400 Pebbly Beach Rd, Avalon, CA 90704

The island's most reliable snorkel spot — a protected, surf-free cove of kelp forest teeming with garibaldi, opaleye and calico bass, a short walk from the ferry dock. Rocky entry, no facilities; rent gear nearby.

Hours: Sun 7am–7pm · Mon 7am–7pm · Tue 7am–7pm · Wed 7am–7pm · Thu 7am–7pm · Fri 7am–7pm · Sat 7am–7pm

South Beach & Middle Beach (Crescent Ave) $

★ 4.4 · Beach & Shoreline · Crescent Ave at Green Pleasure Pier, Avalon, CA 90704

Avalon's two free town beaches flanking the Green Pleasure Pier — calm harbor water, steps from every shop and taco on Crescent Ave. Small and towel-to-towel by 11am on summer weekends; come early for sand.

Hours: Sun 7am–8pm · Mon 7am–8pm · Tue 7am–8pm · Wed 7am–8pm · Thu 7am–8pm · Fri 7am–8pm · Sat 7am–8pm
