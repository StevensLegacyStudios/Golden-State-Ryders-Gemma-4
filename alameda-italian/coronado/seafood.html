Peohe's $$$

★ 4.4 · Seafood · 1201 First St, Coronado, CA 92118

Polynesian-leaning seafood right on the Ferry Landing waterfront with the best downtown-skyline view on the island. The coconut shrimp and weekday happy hour (3-5:30) are the moves.
