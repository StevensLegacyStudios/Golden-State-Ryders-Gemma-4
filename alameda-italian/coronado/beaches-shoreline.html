Coronado Beach (Central Beach) $

★ 4.8 · Beach & Shoreline · Ocean Blvd, Coronado, CA 92118

The island's showpiece: a huge, flat, mica-sparkled strand backed by Ocean Boulevard mansions and the Hotel Del's red turrets, perennially ranked among America's best beaches. Free street parking on Ocean Blvd if you're patient.

Coronado Dog Beach $

★ 4.7 · Beach & Shoreline · Ocean Blvd at Sunset Park (north end), Coronado, CA 92118

The leash-free stretch at the far north end of Ocean Blvd, right against the Naval Air Station fence — one of San Diego's happiest patches of sand. Rinse stations and bags at the Sunset Park entrance.

Silver Strand State Beach $

★ 4.5 · Beach & Shoreline · 5000 Highway 75, Coronado, CA 92118

Two beaches in one down the Strand: ocean surf on one side, warm calm Crown Cove bay water on the other, linked by pedestrian tunnels. Fire rings, big parking lots, and far fewer crowds than Central Beach.

Hours: Sun 8am–7pm · Mon 8am–7pm · Tue 8am–7pm · Wed 8am–7pm · Thu 8am–7pm · Fri 8am–7pm · Sat 8am–7pm
