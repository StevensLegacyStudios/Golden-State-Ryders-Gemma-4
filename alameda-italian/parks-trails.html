Jean Sweeney Open Space Park $

★ 4.6 · Parks & Trails · 1925 Sherman St, Alameda, CA 94501

A 25-acre open-space park built on the old Alameda Belt Line rail yard, with the Cross Alameda Trail running through it, native meadows, a big adventure playground, and covered BBQ picnic areas.

Washington Park $

★ 4.4 · Parks & Trails · 740 Central Ave, Alameda, CA 94501

Big two-section city park a block from Crown Beach: Upper Washington has the playground, rec center, basketball and ballfields; Lower Washington (Otis & Westline) has tennis courts, a fitness trail, and a split large/small dog park.

Bay Farm Island Shoreline Loop (Bay Trail) $

★ 4.7 · Parks & Trails · Veterans Ct & Bay Farm Island Bridge, Alameda, CA 94502

A flat, nearly 6-mile San Francisco Bay Trail loop around Bay Farm Island with nonstop water views of the SF skyline and Bay Bridge — paved path for bikes plus a parallel dirt path for walkers, reached via the Bay Farm bike-pedestrian bridge.

Hours: Sun 5am–10pm · Mon 5am–10pm · Tue 5am–10pm · Wed 5am–10pm · Thu 5am–10pm · Fri 5am–10pm · Sat 5am–10pm
