What this is

Isola is a friendly, independent guide to eating in Alameda, California — the Island. One fast, uncluttered page that answers the only question that matters when you're hungry: who's open right now, and what's on the menu?

For every spot we show live "open now" status (computed in Pacific time), the day's hours, photos, the menu, and one-tap buttons to call, get directions, visit the website, or reserve/order. We cover the whole island — Park Street, Webster Street / West End, South Shore, Marina Village and Bay Farm — across every cuisine.

How we keep it accurate

Listings are compiled from each restaurant's own website and public listings, then checked by hand. Hours, menus and prices still change — especially on holidays — so please call ahead to confirm. Spot something wrong? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

For restaurants

Want to be Featured — pinned to the top and seen first by locals searching "open now"? Founding-sponsor pricing is available for the first spots on the Island. Email us to get featured. Featured placements are always labeled "Featured" so diners know they're sponsored.
