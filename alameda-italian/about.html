What this is

Isola is a friendly, independent guide to Alameda, California — the Island. Restaurants, bars and breweries, beaches and trails, attractions and date-night ideas, all on one fast, uncluttered page that answers the only question that matters when you're heading out: who's open right now?

For every spot we show live "open now" status (computed in Pacific time), the day's hours, menus or highlights with prices, and one-tap buttons to call, get directions, visit the website, or reserve/order. We cover the whole island — Park Street, Webster Street / West End, South Shore, Alameda Point, Marina Village and Bay Farm — from every cuisine to every kind of night out.

How we keep it accurate

Listings are compiled from each venue's own website and public listings, then checked by hand. Hours, menus and prices still change — especially on holidays — so please call ahead to confirm. Spot something wrong? Tell us and we'll fix it fast.

For local businesses

Want to be Featured — pinned to the top and seen first by locals searching "open now"? Founding-sponsor pricing is available for the first spots on the Island. Email us to get featured. Featured placements are always labeled "Featured" so visitors know they're sponsored.
